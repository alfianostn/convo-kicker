Deep Questions for Couples

Reflective questions for the conversations that matter — the ones about trust, growth, and what you are building together. Best asked slowly, one at a time.

  1. What part of our relationship feels strongest to you right now?
  2. When do you feel most emotionally safe with me?
  3. What is something you hope we protect as we grow together?
  4. What future moment are you most excited to share with me?
  5. What truth about love have you learned from us?
  6. What is something you want me to understand about you that is hard to put into words?
  7. How have I changed the way you see yourself?
  8. What does home feel like to you, and do I play a part in that?
  9. Is there a fear you have about us that you have never said out loud?
  10. What is one thing you wish I celebrated more about myself?
  11. When was the first moment you knew I was someone truly special to you?
  12. What does being truly known by someone mean to you?
  13. What is one way we have grown stronger without either of us realizing it?
  14. What is something you have learned about love from watching how I love you?
  15. How do you want us to handle the difficult seasons that will come?
  16. What is a dream of yours I could help make more real?
  17. What is the most loving thing I have ever done for you without knowing it?
  18. What does commitment mean to you beyond just staying together?
  19. Is there anything you have been holding back out of fear of how I might respond?
  20. What is the one thing you want us to always come back to, no matter what?
