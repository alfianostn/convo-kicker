Fun Questions for Friends

Easy, funny questions for hangouts, long drives, or reviving a quiet group chat. No wrong answers — the tangents are the point.

  1. What would our friendship win an award for?
  2. What random memory of us still makes you laugh instantly?
  3. If we started a ridiculous side quest together, what would it be?
  4. What song, snack, or place instantly reminds you of me?
  5. What kind of harmless trouble would be very on brand for us?
  6. If we were a duo in a heist movie, what would our roles be?
  7. What is the most chaotic thing we have done that somehow turned out great?
  8. If our friendship had a mascot, what would it be?
  9. What is something I convinced you to try that you are now secretly glad about?
  10. If we could only communicate in one way for a year, what would we choose?
  11. What is the most unhinged plan we have made and then actually followed through on?
  12. If we had a podcast, what would the first episode be about?
  13. What is a friendship tradition we should start right now?
  14. If you had to describe our friendship using only food, what would you pick?
  15. What is the most dramatically unnecessary thing we have ever done together?
  16. If our friendship was a sport, what would be our signature move?
  17. What is something I have never tried that you think I would be obsessed with?
  18. If we made a documentary about our friendship, what would be the plot twist?
  19. What would you say is our most iconic shared memory?
  20. What is the silliest thing we have argued about that still makes you laugh?
